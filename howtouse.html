How to Use

Step 1

Record Your Audio

Click the "Record" button to start recording your speech. Allow microphone permissions if prompted.

Step 2

Stop Recording

Click the "Stop" button to stop recording when you finish speaking.

Step 3

Upload Audio

Alternatively, you can click the "Upload" button to select an audio file from your device.

Step 4

Get Text Output

After recording or uploading, click the "Submit" button to convert your speech to text. The text will appear in the text box.
